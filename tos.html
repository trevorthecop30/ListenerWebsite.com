Terms of Service & Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 2026

1. Terms of Service

By adding Listener AI to your server, you agree that you will not use the bot for any illegal activities or to violate Discord's own Terms of Service. We reserve the right to restrict access to the bot if these terms are violated.

2. Data Collection (Privacy Policy)

Listener AI is designed with privacy in mind. Here is how we handle your data:

  • User IDs: We store User IDs only when a warning is issued via the !warn command to keep track of server infractions.
  • Messages: We do not log or store your chat messages. The !ask command processes your input in real-time through the Groq API and does not save the conversation history once the response is sent.
  • Server Data: We store basic server IDs and authorized role IDs to ensure the !addroleperm system works correctly.

3. Data Deletion

If you wish for your data (warnings) to be cleared from our system, simply kick the bot from your server or contact Godzilla via the support server.
